My Art

The Hallway

I was very interested in architecture at the time I made this. This is a hallway in my highschool and I made it using shaprie. Using different desgins, such as dots and lines, I was able to add texture and depth to create a sense of what features the hallway had.

Hockey Drawing

A huge part of my life was given to hockey. From moving away from home at an early age, to the early mornings to train. Using pointalism, I wanted to capture a portrait of myself in my comfort zone, in my uniform.

The Hidden Doorway

This is one of my favorite pieces because I made it with no goal in mind. I was simply bored one day and began drawing lines using a ruler. Eventually, I saw door take shape and began to shade in between some of the lines I drew.
