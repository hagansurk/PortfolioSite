Annoucing Highlights

During my time at the University of Michigan, I was able to work for Big Ten Network Student U. This is a student lead broadcasting team for Michigan sports. I was able to work my way up to being a producer, or leader of the whole crew. In part of being a member of BTN StudentU, I had a lot of opportunities to do on-air talent, such as play by play and color commentary, for many different sports. Below you will find clips, highlights, and recordings from various social media sites from my time with BTN.
